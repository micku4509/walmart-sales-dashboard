# 🏪 Walmart Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built in Power BI to simulate a real-world retail business use case.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem

Retail teams were facing multiple challenges:

* No centralized view of sales, profit, and customer performance
* Heavy reliance on manual Excel reports
* Difficulty in comparing performance across regions and product categories
* Delayed decision-making due to lack of real-time insights

---

## ✅ Solution

Designed and developed a **2-page interactive Power BI dashboard** to provide a unified and self-service analytics solution.

### 🔹 Executive Overview

* High-level KPI tracking (Revenue, Transactions, AOV, Customers, Profit Margin)
* Revenue trend analysis over time
* Regional and category-wise performance
* Geographic insights

### 🔹 Product & Customer Insights

* Category and product performance breakdown
* Customer segmentation (Age, Gender, Segment)
* Revenue distribution across different segments
* Detailed product-level analysis

---

## 📊 Data Summary

* **561,000+ transactions** (2020–2023)
* **52 stores** across multiple regions
* Multiple datasets: Sales, Customer, Product, Store, Employee

---

## 🧩 Data Modeling

* Implemented a **Star Schema** for efficient analysis
* Fact Table: `Sales_Fact`
* Dimension Tables: Customer, Product, Store, Employee, Calendar
* Enabled fast filtering and scalable reporting

---

## 📈 Key Features

* Time Intelligence using DAX (YTD, YoY %, Rolling Metrics)
* KPI indicators with conditional formatting (↑ growth / ↓ decline)
* Fully interactive filters (Region, Category, Segment, Age Group, etc.)
* Cross-filtering between visuals for deeper insights
* Clean and intuitive dashboard design

---

## 🎨 Design Approach

* Focused on **clarity and usability**
* Consistent color usage for better readability
* Minimal and structured layout
* Designed for business users, not just technical users
